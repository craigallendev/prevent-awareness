Preventive Awareness Programme

The programme aims to provide awareness and resourceful information that helps to identify the signs of radicalisation earlier and safeguard vulnerable individuals.

Objectives of the Programme

  • Protection
  • Early Intervention
  • Support Network
  • Education and Awareness

Radicalisation

Refers to Individuals adopting extremist ideologies and support or engage in terrorist activities.

Safeguarding

Protecting individuals, especially vulnerable ones, from being drawn into terrorism or extremist activities.

Psychological Impact

  • Exposure to radical ideologies and activities can lead to stress, anxiety, depression and other issues.
  • Struggle between existing beliefs and newly adopted extremist views can cause significant mental conflict.
  • Shift in identity and a heightened sense of belonging to a radical group.
