Support Resources

By partnering with various organizations and providing comprehensive support packages, our Prevent officers have successfully guided many individuals away from extremism and towards a positive path. However, if you feel uncomfortable speaking with us directly, there are several other trusted figures you can turn to. Consider reaching out to a teacher, a local community organization, a faith leader, your local council, or a healthcare professional for guidance. Additionally, if you prefer to start by exploring resources online, there are numerous organizations available to offer advice and support. These resources can provide valuable information and help you find the assistance you need.
